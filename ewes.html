Ewes

Our breeding ewe flock consists of nearly 30 Lincoln ewes. We are developing separate white and natural colored genetic bases, with our white genetic lines heavily influenced by UK genetics.

We select ewes that can give birth unassisted and raise lambs without intervention, ensuring productive and well-balanced sheep with quality fleeces.
